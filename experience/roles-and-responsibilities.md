# Roles & Responsibilities

## Primary Role

**Operations Director & GIS/IT Specialist**

## Responsibility Areas

- Program operations management (technical, administrative, financial)
- GIS/IT systems design and implementation for conservation programs
- Proposal support, reporting, and donor-aligned deliverables
- Field team coordination and multi-site execution
- Workshop, training, and stakeholder engagement facilitation
- Cross-sector liaison with government, NGOs, academic institutions, and community actors

## Delivery Characteristics

- Balanced technical depth with operational execution
- Focused on practical conservation outcomes and community impact
- Built and managed collaboration pipelines across local and international stakeholders
