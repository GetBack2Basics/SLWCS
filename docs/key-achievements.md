# Key Achievements

## Recognition

- Recipient organization of the **2008 Equator Initiative (UNDP) Award** for a leading community-based sustainable development program

## Program Leadership

- Co-developed and scaled **Saving Elephants by Helping People (SEHP)** as a community-integrated conservation model
- Established and operationalized **c2GIS**, a conservation GIS and analytics capability center

## Technical and Systems Contributions

- Developed and applied integrated rapid assessment approaches (including CRES/RERU applications)
- Led implementation of GIS-enabled conservation planning and decision support workflows
- Supported technical methodology design linking biodiversity priorities and socio-economic context

## International Collaboration and Delivery

- Organized and/or delivered conservation workshops, presentations, and technical sessions in Sri Lanka and internationally
- Contributed to specialist-group and cross-sector forums on HEC, protected area strategy, and conservation planning

## Operational Scale

- Managed multi-site teams and cross-functional activities spanning field operations, research support, reporting, and stakeholder engagement
- Oversaw resource planning and administrative coordination across multiple programs and funding streams
