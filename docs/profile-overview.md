# Profile Overview

## Role Scope

**Operations Director & GIS/IT Specialist (SLWCS)**  
Led technical, administrative, and financial management across conservation programs, field operations, GIS systems, and multi-stakeholder initiatives.

## Core Focus Areas

- Human-elephant conflict (HEC) mitigation
- Community-integrated conservation program design
- Conservation GIS and remote sensing systems
- Biodiversity and socio-economic rapid assessments
- Training, workshops, and international technical collaboration

## Context and Period

Primary leadership and delivery period represented in this repository: **1998–2010**.

## Strategic Contributions

- Helped establish **Conservation GIS Center (c2GIS)** as a self-financing technical hub
- Co-led and operationalized **Saving Elephants by Helping People (SEHP)**
- Contributed to development and application of **CRES/RERU** methodology frameworks
- Supported innovation pathways including **eleID** (AI-supported elephant identification) and **eleAlert** (fence intrusion alert system)

## Organizational and Program Impact

- Managed concurrent workstreams across multiple provinces
- Coordinated teams including local community members, researchers, students, and partner institutions
- Supported programs recognized by international funders, collaborators, and conservation networks
