# eleAlert (Electric Fence Intrusion Alert)

## Overview

eleAlert references an electric fence intrusion alert concept/system included in presentation and project records.

## Intended Value

- Strengthen fence monitoring responsiveness
- Support practical conflict mitigation workflows
- Improve operational awareness in high-risk interface zones

## Timeline

- **2009 – 2010** (concept formulated in 2009; prototype design presented in January 2010)

## Budget

- **$200,000 USD** (corrected to 2026 USD; funded through volunteer programs and programmatic operational allocations)


## Impacts & Outcomes

- **Breach Responsiveness**: Developed a prototype system that automatically alerts fence management committees of physical intrusions or voltage drops along boundary lines.
- **Improved Management**: Reduced response times for local communities to repair breaches, significantly lowering crop raiding incidence.
- **Efficiency Gains**: Lowered labor and maintenance costs by replacing manual fence checking schedules with automated real-time alerts.
